How purchases work

Gems are credited to your game profile instantly once payment is confirmed. The game stays free — purchases are optional and cosmetic.

Payments are handled by our payment provider and Merchant of Record, Paddle, who is the seller of record and handles billing and applicable taxes (e.g. VAT). Your purchase is also subject to Paddle's terms shown at checkout.

Gems and in-game items have no real-world monetary value and are not redeemable for cash. Purchases are generally non-refundable once Gems are credited, except where required by law — see the Purchases & Refund Policy. Prices may change.
