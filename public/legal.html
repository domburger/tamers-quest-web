Legal & Privacy

How Tamer's Quest (tamersquest.com) handles your data, the terms for playing, and who runs it.

Plain-language summary

  • You play with a nickname you choose — no email, password, or real name required.
  • We keep an anonymous session token (a random code, stored in your browser and on our server) so your progress comes back when you return. It is not linked to your identity.
  • We store your game progress (your monsters, gold, upgrades, and stats) on our server so it persists between sessions.
  • We use browser local storage for essentials (your session, mute setting, onboarding flag, equipped cosmetic) — no tracking or advertising cookies.
  • Combat and monster generation use the OpenAI API; the game is hosted on Railway. We don't sell your data.
  • Your nickname and extraction / PvP-win counts may appear on the public leaderboard.

Cookie & Storage Notice

Tamer's Quest does not use cookies for tracking or advertising, and embeds no third-party analytics or ad trackers. It uses your browser's localStorage for a small number of strictly necessary values:

KeyPurpose
tq_session_tokenYour anonymous session token — links your browser to your saved game profile.
tq_mutedRemembers whether you muted the game's sound.
tq_onboardedRemembers that you've seen the first-run how-to-play hint, so it isn't shown again.
tq_chain_skinRemembers which (visual-only) spirit-chain cosmetic you equipped.
tq_admin_tokenOperator-only: stored on the admin panel for site administrators; not set during normal play.

Because these are essential to the Service's basic operation, they are used without a consent banner. Clearing them simply resets the corresponding preference (and, for the session token, your link to your anonymous profile).
